Vietnam

In July 2018, My family & I visited Vietnam & Cambodia for two weeks.
We visited many parts of Vietnam including Hanoi, Hoi An, Ho Chi Minh and Ha Long Bay.
Vietnam is enriched with History following The Vietnam War.


The weather was extremely humid due to the time of year that we visited,
It was extremely eye-opening and fascinating to experience such a different culture to our own.
Vietnam was amazing and I hope to travel Asia a lot more when I am older.
