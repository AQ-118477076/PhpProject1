Aghada Ladies Gaelic Football Club

I am a proud member of Aghada Ladies Gaelic football Club.
I have been playing Football with Aghada since I was six years old,
I love being part of a team as i have grown up playing and going to school with the girls on my team.


Our team has had a very successful two years, in 2017 we won the Junior All Ireland Final,
We have since progressed from an intermediate team to a senior team in the last year,
Being part of a team is an amazing team and is an even better feeling when you are winning as a team.
